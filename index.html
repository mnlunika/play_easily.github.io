To play well, you just need to know
what keys to press.

Johann Sebestian Bach

Once on a TV show, one very famous russian film actor asked
what was the most surprising and incredible for him, answered
that this is how a person can play on a musical instrument.
For me it was very strange, since he graduated a very famous school of actors where great music teachers were teaching.

Quite recently, I learned from the Internet that out of the millions
of Amerikans who are starting to learn the playing on musical instruments
at the end of the year, only 20% remains. The 80% of them are so stupid? And they must still study for 2-3 years...

Three years at school we had lessons on music, but no one could play after school graduation.

Now I know that just one lesson is enough to set forth the right information on music.

Recently, many books and articles on the Internet have appeared on this topic. I reviewed a lot of them. The same garbage as before. Therefore, we wrote three books, which outlined the structure of music and simple rules, knowing that you can easily play on any instrument:

  • "Music Theory for Teenagers"
  • "Play Easilly on Piano and Keyboards"
  • "You Can Play Guitar in Few Weeks"

For this, special abilities are not needed and they should not be developed from early childhood
